Game Modes

This is dependent content

Multiplayer session battles are battles where two teams, each of which has from 6 to 16 players, fight each other, performing tasks - capturing airfields/bases, defending/attacking territories. In addition to completing the task, the battle also ends if all vehicles in one of the teams have been destroyed and/or all players have left the battle.

Single and cooperative missions are battles where the enemy is equipment controlled by artificial intelligence. The player's task can be both the destruction of all enemy vehicles and the completion of special tasks

WarThunder Ship

Realistic mode - there are no markers over players, replenishment of weapons, as well as automatic range correction to the enemy. Vehicle speed decreased.

WarThunder Airplane

The arcade mode is the easiest to learn. There are: a lead marker, markers over aircraft, a 3rd person view, replenishment of shells in flight, a simpler flight model. Battles are fast and dynamic. You can respawn on all aircraft of the setup 1 time (2 times, if there are backups). Representatives of the same nation can get into different teams.

WarThunder boat

The arcade mode, similar to air and tank battles, is designed for beginners and is easy to learn. Vehicles have increased speed characteristics, markers are displayed above all players, automatic range correction, and lead display for large-caliber weapons. Spent ammo is reloaded after a short time.

WarThunder Tank

The arcade mode is the easiest to learn. There are: markers over all tanks, ballistics and penetration probability indicator, increased speed and maneuverability of tanks, the ability to continue moving after 10 seconds. after the engine breakdown without repair, but with a penalty in power. There are 3 tank respawns available. During the game, respawn points are accumulated for aircraft, when used, the player is given a random aircraft of the selected class that matches the Battle Rating. Representatives of the same nation can get into different teams.
